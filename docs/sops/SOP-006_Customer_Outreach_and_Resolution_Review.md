# SOP-006 Customer Outreach and Resolution Review

## Purpose

Define when customer outreach or resolution review is appropriate in the synthetic workflow.

## Scope

This SOP applies to customer reviews, customer-reported concerns, palatability concerns, suspected ADE clarification, product-quality concerns, and cases where information is needed before final handling.

## Review Procedure

1. Determine whether the record is a customer review or a QRE/general-question form submission.
2. For customer reviews, use star rating and review-text presence to decide whether outreach is expected.
3. For QRE/general-question submissions, determine whether the needed information is already available to frontline pharmacists or requires Technical Services follow-up.
4. Separate customer outreach from final handling path.
5. Separate customer-facing resolution options from final handling path.

## Customer Review Rules

One- to three-star customer reviews always have a star rating. Review text is optional. If a one- to three-star review has no text, perform record review and document if no quality concern is found. If a one- to three-star review has text, perform record review and consider customer outreach. Four- and five-star reviews do not require routine follow-up unless the text suggests a quality or safety concern.

## Resolution Options

Resolution review may include replacement or reship review, refund or concession review, alternate dosage form discussion, counseling or follow-up, leadership-directed resolution, or no customer-facing resolution.

## Frontline Pharmacist Questions

Frontline pharmacist questions usually do not require customer outreach unless the pharmacist request includes a customer-reported concern that cannot be answered with available frontline guidance.

## Notes and Limitations

The synthetic system may suggest that outreach or resolution review is appropriate. It does not issue refunds, ship replacements, contact customers, or document real calls.
